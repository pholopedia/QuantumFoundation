Nuclear Magnetic Resonance

When gyromagnetic ratio is negative, it means that the spin and magnetic moment point in the opposite direcitons.

Gyromagnetic ratio of a particle or system is the ratio of its magnetic moment to its angular momentum.

It is denoted by gamma γ [rad / s*T] or γ / 2π [MHz / T]
